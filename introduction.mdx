ExcaliDash is a self-hosted dashboard and organizer for [Excalidraw](https://github.com/excalidraw/excalidraw) drawings. Instead of losing sketches across browser tabs or local files, ExcaliDash gives you a central place to save, search, and organize everything — with real-time collaboration built in. You run it on your own infrastructure, so your data stays where you put it.

## Who it's for

ExcaliDash is for individuals, teams, and homelab operators who use Excalidraw regularly and want durable, searchable storage without relying on a third-party cloud service. It suits anyone who needs multi-user access, wants to enforce authentication, or simply prefers owning their own data.

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Persistent drawing storage" icon="floppy-disk">
    All your drawings are saved automatically in a SQLite-backed store. No more lost work between browser sessions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real-time collaboration" icon="users">
    Multiple users can edit the same drawing simultaneously with live presence — powered by Socket.IO.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Collections" icon="folder">
    Drag and drop drawings into named collections to keep projects and topics organized.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Search" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Find any drawing by name across your entire library instantly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Export and import" icon="arrow-right-arrow-left">
    Back up and restore drawings in the open `.excalidraw` format — no proprietary lock-in.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Scoped sharing" icon="share-nodes">
    Share individual drawings internally or generate external links with controlled access.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Flexible authentication" icon="lock">
    Use local email/password login, OIDC single sign-on, or enforce OIDC-only access for your organization.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Admin controls" icon="shield-halved">
    Bootstrap a first admin account, manage users, and recover access via the built-in admin dashboard.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
